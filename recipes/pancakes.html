Buttermilk pancakes recipe

Buttermilk Pancakes preparation

As convenient as a store-bought mix can be, every home cook needs a good buttermilk pancake recipe in their repertoire. Don't have a tried-and-true favorite yet? You're in luck! I and my loved ones can't get enough of these classic buttermilk pancakes. Friends i shared with say they're light, fluffy, moist, and full of rich flavor.

This recipe is very easy, and the ingredients are not costly! Below i will be showing you every ingredients you need, and preparation methods.

Take your time, and Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • All-purpose Flour
  • White Sugar
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients

    2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.

  2. Mix Wet Ingredients

    3. Beat the buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter together in a separate bowl. Do not combine the wet and dry ingredients until right before you cook the pancakes.

  3. Make The Pancakes

    4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix with a wooden spoon or fork until the mixtures are just-blended. Scoop the batter onto a hot, oiled griddle and cook until the pancake is bubbly on the top. Flip with a spatula and cook until both sides are brown.

There's nothing like plain buttermilk pancakes topped with syrup, you could also top your buttermilk pancakes with jam or honey for a sweet twist on a classic.

Now your Buttermilk Pancakes are incredibly light and fluffy, just.. Enjoy!!
